# Stakeholders

## Purpose

Project Atlas is designed to support multiple business users across a fictional multi-warehouse distribution company. Each stakeholder has different operational goals, reporting needs, and key performance indicators (KPIs).

Understanding these stakeholders helps ensure that the platform is designed around business value rather than technology alone.

---

# Stakeholder Overview

| Stakeholder | Primary Responsibilities | Atlas Value |
|--------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| Executive Leadership | Monitor company-wide operational performance | Executive dashboards, KPIs, trends, inventory valuation |
| Operations Manager | Oversee warehouse efficiency and inventory flow | Warehouse performance metrics, stock levels, operational trends |
| Procurement Manager | Manage supplier relationships and purchase orders | Supplier scorecards, purchase order tracking, delivery performance |
| Inventory Control Analyst | Maintain inventory accuracy | Inventory reconciliation, cycle count analysis, discrepancy reporting |
| Warehouse Supervisor | Manage daily warehouse operations | Receiving activity, inventory movements, transfer monitoring |
| Finance Analyst | Track inventory valuation and purchasing costs | Inventory value reporting, purchasing trends, cost analysis |

---

# Stakeholder Goals

## Executive Leadership

Needs to answer questions such as:

- How healthy is our inventory?
- Which warehouses are performing best?
- Are inventory costs increasing?
- Where are operational risks developing?

Primary dashboards:

- Executive Overview
- Inventory Value
- Company KPIs

---

## Operations Manager

Needs to understand:

- Warehouse throughput
- Receiving efficiency
- Inventory availability
- Stockouts
- Overstock situations

Primary dashboards:

- Warehouse Operations Dashboard
- Receiving Dashboard

---

## Procurement Manager

Needs visibility into:

- Supplier performance
- Purchase order status
- Late deliveries
- Vendor reliability
- Purchasing trends

Primary dashboards:

- Procurement Dashboard
- Supplier Scorecards

---

## Inventory Control Analyst

Responsible for:

- Inventory reconciliation
- Inventory adjustments
- Cycle counts
- Negative inventory detection
- Data quality monitoring

Primary dashboards:

- Inventory Accuracy Dashboard
- Data Quality Dashboard

---

## Warehouse Supervisor

Focuses on daily warehouse activities including:

- Receiving shipments
- Inventory transfers
- Employee productivity
- Warehouse workload

Primary dashboards:

- Receiving Dashboard
- Inventory Movement Dashboard

---

## Finance Analyst

Responsible for:

- Inventory valuation
- Purchase spending
- Cost trends
- Financial reporting support

Primary dashboards:

- Inventory Valuation Dashboard
- Purchasing Cost Dashboard

---

# Design Principle

Every stakeholder identified in Project Atlas should have at least one business requirement, one supporting dataset, and one dashboard or report that helps them make better decisions.
