# Company Profile

## Purpose

This document defines the fictional organization that Project Atlas is built around.

Rather than using anonymous or randomly generated data, Atlas simulates the operations of a realistic distribution company. This provides meaningful business context for the database design, SQL development, ETL pipelines, reporting, and Power BI dashboards throughout the project.

---

# Company Mission

Atlas Distribution Company provides reliable industrial inventory solutions by combining efficient warehouse operations, disciplined procurement practices, and data-driven decision making to help customers receive the right products at the right time.

---

# Company Vision

To become the most operationally efficient industrial distributor in the southeastern United States through continuous improvement, technology, and business intelligence.

---

# Company Overview

**Company Name:** Atlas Distribution Company

**Industry:** Industrial Distribution & Supply Chain Operations

**Headquarters:** Orlando, Florida

**Business Model:**
Atlas Distribution Company purchases industrial products from approved suppliers and distributes inventory across multiple warehouse locations to support operational departments and customer demand.

The company focuses on inventory accuracy, supplier performance, warehouse efficiency, and data-driven decision-making.

---
