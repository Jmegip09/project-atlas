### Vision Established

- **Objective:** Transform the CS-499 Inventory Management Capstone into an enterprise-style Supply Chain Intelligence Platform.
- **Target Technology Stack:** PostgreSQL, Python (Pandas/ETL), Power BI, AWS Cloud, Snowflake, and dbt.
- **Business Focus:** Simulate real-world inventory management, warehouse operations, procurement, supplier performance, and inventory reconciliation processes.
- **Long-Term Goal:** Build a continuously evolving platform that demonstrates software engineering, data analytics, business intelligence, and data engineering skills through one cohesive project.
